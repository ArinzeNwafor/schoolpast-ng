Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Get Started" button and following the registration process. It's quick and free!

Is Schoolpast.ng free to use?

Yes, Schoolpast.ng is free for all Nigerian students. We offer premium features for additional functionality.

How can I access past questions?

After creating an account, you can browse our extensive library of past questions organized by institution, course, and year.

How do I apply for scholarships?

You can browse available scholarships in the Financial Aid section and follow the application instructions for each opportunity.
