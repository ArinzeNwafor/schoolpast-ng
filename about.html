Our Story

Schoolpast.ng was founded with a vision to revolutionize the educational experience for Nigerian students through technology, community, and transparency.

After witnessing firsthand the challenges faced by students across Nigeria, from limited access to quality educational materials to inadequate financial support, Arinze set out to create a comprehensive solution that addresses these issues.

Today, Schoolpast.ng stands as an innovative digital platform designed to serve as the voice and hub for Nigerian students, amalgamating educational resources, social networking, institutional feedback mechanisms, and financial aid opportunities into a single, user-friendly application.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian students by providing a unified digital platform that integrates academic resources, social engagement, institutional accountability, and financial support, fostering an inclusive and proactive student community.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading student-centric platform, revolutionizing the educational experience through technology, community, and transparency.

The Problem

Nigerian students face challenges such as:

  • Limited access to quality educational materials
  • Lack of platforms to voice concerns
  • Inadequate financial support
  • Minimal engagement with institutional policies

Our Solution

Schoolpast.ng addresses these issues by providing:

  • Access to past examination questions and study materials
  • A social platform for student interaction and expression
  • Mechanisms to report institutional issues and rate staff
  • Opportunities for scholarships, donations, and financial aid
