What We Give You

Discover the powerful tools and resources that make Schoolpast.ng the ultimate platform for Nigerian students

Educational Resources

Access a comprehensive library of study materials, past questions, and learning resources to excel in your academic journey.

  • Past examination questions from various institutions
  • Comprehensive study guides and notes
  • Video tutorials and explanations
  • Practice tests and quizzes
  • Downloadable textbooks and reference materials

Social Networking

Connect with peers, join study groups, and share knowledge in a vibrant student community.

  • Create and join study groups based on courses or interests
  • Participate in academic discussions and forums
  • Connect with students from your institution or others
  • Share notes, resources, and study tips
  • Collaborate on projects and assignments

Institutional Feedback

Provide feedback to institutions and improve education quality through transparent reporting.

  • Rate and review courses and instructors
  • Submit anonymous feedback on institutional services
  • Report issues and suggest improvements
  • View aggregated feedback and ratings
  • Track institutional responses and changes

Financial Aid

Discover scholarships, grants, and financial opportunities to support your educational goals.

  • Comprehensive database of scholarships and grants
  • Personalized scholarship recommendations
  • Application guidance and tips
  • Deadline reminders and notifications
  • Success stories and testimonials

Student Petitions

Create and support petitions to drive positive change in your institution and the Nigerian education system.

  • Launch petitions for institutional reforms and improvements
  • Support causes that matter to the student community
  • Track petition progress and institutional responses
  • Connect with like-minded students across institutions
  • Amplify your voice through collective action
